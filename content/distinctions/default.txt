## Alladii vs Sirniki
Alladii are made with kefir. Sirniki with farmers cheese.

## Nalisniki vs blini
Blini are empty. Nalisniki are the same thing but with  a filling. 

## Pelmeni vs varenini vs pierogi 
Pelmeni = boiled, with raw meat
Vareniki = boiled, with precooked meat
Pierogi = baked
